What does car insurance cover?

Car insurance covers damages to your vehicle, third-party liability, and personal injury. Comprehensive plans may also include protection against fire, theft, and natural disasters.

What are the different types of car insurance?

The two main types are third-party insurance and comprehensive insurance, covering damage to both your vehicle and third parties.

How can I file a car insurance claim?

To file a claim, notify your insurer, submit necessary documents, and complete their claims process.

What factors affect my car insurance premium?

Premiums depend on factors like car make and model, age, driver’s history, and chosen coverage type.

What is the IDV in car insurance?

IDV is the maximum sum insured by the insurer in case of total loss or theft, calculated based on market value.
